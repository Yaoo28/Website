About Palawan

The Tabon and Palawan Museums bring the areas' local history to life. The province has some of the most exciting and challenging dive sites in the world. Horse riding and inland trekking are 'must-do's' for those who prefer a slower-paced experience. Palawan consists of 1,780 islands of pristine white beaches, dramatic rock formations, secret coves, and underground mysteries. UNESCO has sited Tubbataha Reef and the Saint Paul Subterranean River National Park as World Heritage sites. Palawan was also named "World's Best Island"
